Quick Start Guide for Saliva Testing Kit

This guide is intended to help you get started quickly. If you would like additional assistance, please contact us at 888-905-0226 or email us at support@dentulu.com

Purchasing the Oralgenetix Kit

You can purchase oralgenetix kit at www.oralgenetix.com Follow the on screen instructions to place an order and complete the sign up process. Once you have completed the order placement you will be redirected to the dashboard to track your order status of your kit and as soon as reports are available.

How to use the kit

Open the box received. The box will contain the following items.

  • Collection Tube - 2 Nos.
  • Funnel - 1 Nos.
  • Return Shipping Label - 1 Nos.
  • Name Tag - 2 Nos.
  • Fedex Clinical Pak
  • Instruction Manual

Write your name and date of birth on the provided barcode labels.

SWISH and GARGLE for 30 seconds with provided saline solution. NOTE: Specimen should be collected prior to cleaning (e.g. debridement or rinsing with antimicrobials).

Kindly EXPECTORATE into labeled collection tube. Remove and discard funnel and seal collection tube with provided red cap.

Affix one label VERTICALLY ON THE SPECIMEN TUBE – PLEASE DO NOT WRAP THE LABEL AROUND THE TUBE - THIS WILL PRECLUDE US FROM READING THE BAR CODE CORRECTLY. (The second label is optional and may be used to label your records or used if the 1st label is destroyed).

Place capped sample tube into provided plastic zip seal Specimen Bag with absorbent pad. Seal bag and place bagged specimen back into corrugated box in preparation for shipment.

Package and Shipping Specimen

Place the specimen box and folded shipping manifest/s inside the provided FedEx Clinical Pak and place the FedEx label on the front (Note: FedEx Clinical Paks require a scheduled pickup; they cannot be placed in drop boxes. Call 800-463-3339 or visit FedEx.com/us/oadr to setup your free FedEx account).
